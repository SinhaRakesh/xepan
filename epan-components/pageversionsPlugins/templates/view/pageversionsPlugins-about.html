Page Version (A/B/__) Testing

Page version allows you to deliver various page versions (snapshots) to your visitor in a sequential manner. Each of your offered snapshot is rendered to your visitor and a history is managed. You can attach various goals with the page. Later, you can have an analysis about the goals achieved by various versions.

This will gives you a better look inside your layout and punch lines or even your showcase products. You can vary between various layouts and combinations to try.
